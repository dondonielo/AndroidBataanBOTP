HOW TO PLAY

Bataan: The Battle of the Philippines

The Playing Field of the Game

The playing field is made up of 67 points, 17 American Flag and 50 Japanese Flag. It also consist of 14 blocks for both piece is prohibited on that cell. The 3 Americans (blue piece) men are set up in the white on the starting points and the 50 Japanese (yellow piece) on the 50 Japanese Flags.

Objective of the Game

The objective of the game is for the Japanese Field Army(yellow piece) to get control of the Defense Area (the 17 American flag) by having an army on each point. The American Defense Army(blue piece) must stop the Field Army. Only the American Defense Army (blue piece) can remove enemies by jumping over an occupied point into an empty point as in checkers and multiple jumps are allowed. The blue armies can only be removed if they refuse a jump.The blue and yellow piece armies can both move one step in all directions provided there is an empty destination cell.

Game Victory

Victory occurs when either the Japanese Field Army occupies all 17 American Flag in the Defense Area(Japanese wins) or when it becomes impossible for them to do so (the Defense army kills more than 33 Japanese men, American wins). The game will also end if there are no Americans left (if it is all removed) and the Japanese wins. Also, a draw occurs when all of the remaining American Defense Army are surrounded by the Japanese Field Army and it cannot make any move provided that the Japanese Field Army has not occuppied the defense area.
